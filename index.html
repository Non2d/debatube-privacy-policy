Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

DebaTube does not collect or store any personal information on external servers.

2. Microphone Access

DebaTube requests access to your device's microphone to enable in-app voice features. Audio is temporarily transmitted to a local Whisper speech recognition server for transcription purposes only. The transcribed text is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third parties, except as described in Section 4.

3. Use of Information

We do not sell, trade, or upload any user data to our servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

DebaTube uses the Google Gemini API to provide AI-powered features. Transcribed text from your speech may be sent to Google Gemini for processing. No audio data is sent to Google. This data is handled in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

5. Children's Privacy

DebaTube does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
